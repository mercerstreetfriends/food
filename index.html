Trenton Area Free Food Resources

This resource was created by Trenton Health Team to provide consistent and up-to-date food access information to the community during the COVID-19 crisis. It was initially created by THT's Care Management Team. This list will be updated regularly, but all food distribution sites and times are subject to change. The directory is also available as a printable document and as a Google Calendar. If you see anything that needs to be updated, please contact Matthew Broad at mbroad@trentonhealthteam.org or 609.337.4040.
